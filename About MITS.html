Chai Trivia

When did Chai first appear in The New zealand?

James Cook and his crewmen were the first Europeans to drink tea, and Cook referred to mānuka as 'The Tea Plant' in his journal. Tea became steadily cheaper throughout the 19th century as New Zealand's population grew from 2009. (source:NZ History Magazine)


When and who discovered the chai first?

An early legend is that a monk traveling to China over 5000 years ago noticed the locals chewing on the leaves of a bush and discovered its energetic properties. Another legend has it that it was a king in ancient India (most likely Harshavardhana) who developed chai to remain alert during long hours in court. (source:The Hindu Magazine)


What is the use of Drinking Chai?

Chai tea is a fragrant, spicy tea that may help boost heart health, reduce blood sugar levels, aid digestion and help with weight loss.Chai may help to reduce wrinkles and fight acne,help to increase metabolism, and therefore help to lose weight. (source:Clean Eating Magazine)


What type of chai reduces reduces Headache and cold?

Masala chai can reduce the headache or cold. The spices in masala chai, particularly ginger and cardamom, have been shown to have anxiolytic properties that can help reduce stress and anxiety. (source:Golden Tips Tea Magazine)
